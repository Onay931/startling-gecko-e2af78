What We Collect

Professional Recycling of
Industrial Electrical Components

We specialise in components that most recyclers overlook — the high-value electrical gear that deserves proper recovery, not landfill.

⚡

Contactors & Relays

Old contactors and motor starters from workshops, factories, and panel rooms. All sizes collected — from small DIN-rail units to heavy industrial frames.

🔌

Electrical Breakers

MCBs, MCCBs, ACBs, and air circuit breakers. Whether surplus, end-of-life, or removed during upgrades — we collect and recycle responsibly.

🗄️

Control Panels & Boards

Full panel clearances for factory strip-outs, building conversions, and electrical room decommissioning. We clear the room so you don't have to.

❄️

HVAC Components

Motor starters, chiller contactors, and control gear from HVAC systems. Building plant rooms and cooling tower installations are a speciality.

🏗️

Elevator & Lift Parts

Old open-frame contactors from lift motor rooms, pulled during modernisations. We work directly with elevator maintenance companies.

🏭

Motor Rewind Scrap

Ongoing partnerships with motor rewinding workshops. We set up a regular weekly or monthly collection route — no sorting, no hassle.
